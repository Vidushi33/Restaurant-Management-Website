ABOUT US

Pine and dine is located in the heart of delhi. From family-style dining to riffs of indian street food, our menu balances intense fresh flavours with seasonal ingredients and locally sourced products. our menu is served alongside an inspired mocktail card, which uses fresh fruits and no artificial flavours or preserved fruit essense is used ,no compromise is done with the flavours , Connecting people to place through contemporary indian cuisine, pine and dine crafts inspired dishes reflective of our country’s diverse landscape, history and culture. Our chefs work closely with farmers and suppliers to source the finest ingredients from across the nation. With over 20 years of culinary history, Canoe continues to challenge creative boundaries in the spirit of celebrating india and its distinct provinces. "Creating a memorable experience is what we strive for. From corporate business functions, family get togethers, wine dinners or extravagant affairs, it is our utmost pleasure to accommodate. We also offer the option of a full restaurant buyout for seated dinners or standing receptions. Please contact our events coordinator for additional details or to set up a tour of our space.

SOME SPECIAL ITEMS

Butter Chicken

Chunks of chicken tikka cooked in rich tomatoe gravy and cream with the blended flavours of indian spices in the currry and topped with butter and cream with a touch of sourness and a perfect dish to go with butter naan .

Kadai paneer

Indian cottage cheese cooked in gravy with five spice mix and chunks of vegetable like onion , tomatoe and capsisum, rich gravy with a little tangy punch finished with little bit of corriander .

Dhosa

Indian version of pancakes made with urad daal , the healthiest and the tastiest option to eat stuffed with mashed potato and cottage cheese with a little bit of spices, served with a lentil soup brouth with vegies and coconut chutney .

Manchurian

Manchurian Chicken/Prawn/Fish/Mutton, consisting of a variety of meats or paneer with vegetables in a spicy brown sauce which consist of soya sauce, vinegar and other Chinese spices.

Chicken Tikka

Boneless chicken traditionally baked in a tandoor oven then coated in a creamy tomato sauce,the tikka masala combines the creaminess of milder curries with a hint of spice from hotter variants to form the perfect medium dish. The richness of traditional spices makes the tikka a delicious snack.

Kitkat Shake

Rich creamy shake for kitkat lovers , thick shake with chunks of kitkat combined with ice cream and blended with the finest choclates.
